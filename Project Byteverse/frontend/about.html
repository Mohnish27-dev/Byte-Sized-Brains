Why We Exist

Every year, 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted globally while nearly 10% of the world's population goes hungry. RescueBite was created to bridge this gap by providing a platform that connects food businesses with surplus food to charities and individuals in need.

40% of food produced in the US goes uneaten
42M Americans face food insecurity
25% of freshwater used to produce wasted food

How RescueBite Works

1

Food Businesses List Surplus

Restaurants, grocery stores, and other food businesses post their surplus food on our platform with details about type, quantity, and pickup time.

2

Charities Get Notified

Registered charities and organizations receive alerts about available food in their area that matches their needs.

3

Food Gets Collected

The charity arranges pickup or delivery of the food items, ensuring they reach people in need quickly.

4

Impact is Tracked

Both donors and recipients can see the positive impact they're making through our reporting dashboard.
